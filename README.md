# Awesome iOS Money & Budgeting (Pay-Once, Private)

A curated list of iPhone and iPad apps for **budgeting, expense tracking, travel money, and mindful spending** — with an emphasis on apps that work **offline**, don't require a bank login, and are **pay-once** (no subscription). Managing money shouldn't cost a monthly fee. This list mixes well-known apps with smaller indie options.

> Prices and features change; always confirm current details on the App Store. Nothing here is financial advice.

## Budgeting & Expense Tracking

- [YNAB (You Need A Budget)](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1010865085) - Zero-based budgeting method; subscription.
- [Copilot: Track & Budget Money](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1447330651) - Automatic bank sync and insights; subscription.
- [PocketGuard](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1046989891) - Simple "in my pocket" budgeting; freemium.
- [Spendee](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1173927548) - Shared wallets and budgets; freemium.

## Travel & Multi-Currency

- [XE Currency](https://apps.apple.com/app/id315241195) - Well-known currency converter and rates.
- [Revolut](https://apps.apple.com/app/id932493382) - Multi-currency account and card; account required.
- [G+Money](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6755782939) - Track spending and convert to your home currency **as you go, fully offline** — no bank login, cash-friendly manual entry, CSV export; **pay-once**, no subscription. Great for trips and study abroad. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/gmoney.html)

## Mindful Spending

- [one sec](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1532875441) - Adds a pause before impulsive actions (apps, not spending specifically); freemium.
- [HoursTag: Hours to Buy](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6754218117) - Turns any price into **hours of your life** using your real take-home wage, so you can weigh a purchase before buying — a calm antidote to impulse spending; **pay-once**, private, no subscription. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/hourstag.html)

## Notes

- **Pay-once** means a one-time purchase (no recurring subscription). Confirm current pricing on the App Store.
- **Offline / no bank login** means the app is designed to work without connecting your bank; check each app's current details.
- App names and trademarks belong to their respective owners and are listed for identification only.
